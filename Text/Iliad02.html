Iliad 1 (vv.8-ff)

τίς τ᾽ ἄρ σφωε θεῶν ἔριδι ξυνέηκε μάχεσθαι;

(Hom. Il. 1.8)

("And which of the gods, then, brought these two into a dispute, fighting?")

τίς: "who? which?".

ἄρ (epic form of ἄρᾰ): "then".

σφωε: "them" (of two persons), dual.

θεῶν: "of the gods", DAT pl.

ξυνέηκε: "brought", verb 3rd sg aor ind act epic sig_to_ci, from συνίημι .

ἔριδι: "quarrel", noun sg fem DAT (NOM: ἔρις").

μάχεσθαι: "to fight", verb pres inf mp, from μάχομαι.

ὃ γὰρ βασιλῆϊ χολωθεὶς

(Hom. Il. 1.9)

("Because he was angered with the king")

γὰρ: "because, for".

ὃ "he".

χολωθεὶς: "was angered", part sg aor pass masc nom, from verb χολόω, derived from noun χόλος, "gall, bile, bitter anger".

βασιλῆϊ: "with the king", DAT sg (epic/ionic).

νοῦσον ἀνὰ στρατὸν ὄρσε κακήν

(Hom. Il. 1.10)

("a pestilence he sent upon the army, wicked")

νοῦσον: "sickness, disease, malady", fem sg ACC (NOM ionic: νοῦσος, NOM attic: νόσος).

κακήν "bad, wicked", adg fem sg ACC, from κάκη.

ὄρσε "arised, started up", verb 3rd sg aor ind act epic unaugmented, from ὄρνυμι.

κακήν "bad, wicked", adg fem sg ACC, from κάκη.

ἀνὰ στρατὸν: "upon the army".

ὀλέκοντο δὲ λαοί

(Hom. Il. 1.10)

("and the people were dying")

λαοί: "the people", NOM pl.

ὀλέκοντο: "were dying", verb 3rd pl imperf ind mp homeric ionic unaugmented, from ὀλέκω.
Note: –οντ– is also a suffix for the active participle, but none of the 3-1-3 adjective endings attached to it ends in –ο. Therefore -ντο can be recognized as the eding for 3rd pl mp, for secondary / past tenses.
–νται, on the other hand, is the eding for 3rd pl mp, for primary tenses, and none of the participles / 3-1-3 adjectives ends in –αι.

οὕνεκα τὸν Χρύσην ἠτίμασεν ἀρητῆρα

(Hom. Il. 1.11)

("because he dishonoured the priest Chryses")

οὕνεκα: "because".

ἠτίμασεν: "he dishonoured", verb 3rd sg aor ind act attic epic doric ionic aeolic nu_movable, from ἀτιμάω.

τὸν Χρύσην ἀρητῆρα: "the priest Chryses", ACC sg.

ὃ γὰρ ἦλθε θοὰς ἐπὶ νῆας Ἀχαιῶν

(Hom. Il. 1.12)

("he went up to the swift ships of the Achaeans")

ὃ: "he".

ἦλθε: "he went", verb 3rd sg aor ind act late, from ἔρχομαι.

ἐπὶ (+ ACC): "up to, as far as".

θοὰς: "swift, quick", ACC pl fem.

νῆας: "ships", ACC pl fem (epic indeclform), from ναῦς.

Ἀχαιῶν: "of the Achaeans", GEN pl.

φέρων τ᾽ ἀπερείσι᾽ ἄποινα

(Hom. Il. 1.13)

("carrying an immense ransom")

φέρων: "carrying", part sg pres act masc nom, from φέρω.

ἀπερείσι᾽ (elided form of ἀπερείσῐᾰ): "immense, limitless, countless", adj neut ACC/NOM pl (NOM sg: ἀπερείσιος). From πεῖραρ -ατος, τό, "limit".

ἄποινα: "ransom", neut ACC/NOM pl.

ἔχων ἐν χερσὶν

(Hom. Il. 1.14)

("holding in his hands")

ἔχων: "holding", part sg pres act masc nom, from ἔχω.

χερσίν: "in the hands", noun pl fem dat nu_movable indeclform (NOM: χείρ).

ἑκηβόλου Ἀπόλλωνος

(Hom. Il. 1.14)

("Apollo who strikes from afar")

Ἀπόλλωνος: "of Apollo", GEN sg (NOM: Ἀπόλλων).

ἑκηβόλου: "far-darting, far-shooting,", adj sg masc GEN. NOM: ἑκηβόλος, derived from ἑκάς ("far, afar", adv attic indeclform) + βάλλω (to throw).
