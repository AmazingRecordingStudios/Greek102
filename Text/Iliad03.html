Iliad 3 (vv.108-121)

ἐσθλὸν | δ᾽ οὔτέ τί πω | εἶπες ἔπος | οὐδ᾽ ἐτέλεσσας good | and not any so far | you said word | nor brought to pass

(Hom. Il. 1.108)

("but a word of good you have never yet spoken, nor brought to pass")

ἐσθλὸν : "good" (much like ἀγαθός), adj sg neut nom/acc (nom masc: ἐσθλός ).
δ᾽ (elided form of δέ ): "and, but", conjunction.
οὔτέ: οὔτέ_(peri22b).mp3
τί : "any", pron sg neut acc enclitic indeclform, from τις
πω: πω_(peri22b)
εἶπες : "you said", verb 2nd sg aor ind act, from 1st sg aor: εἶπον ("I said").
Originally it started with a digamma, as in the Cretan dialect: ϝεῖπαι (aor inf act).
The verb λέγω ("I say"), is less commonly used in its aorist form, for which εἶπον is more common, and in the future, future passive, and perfect, for which forms of εἴρω are more often used.
In εἶπον and εἶπες we find the typical –ον ending of the 1st and 2nd pers active of –ω verbs.
ἔπος : "word", noun sg neut acc/nom.
Originally, as in the Arcadocypriot dialect, it started with a digamma: ϝέπος .
οὐδ᾽: οὐδ᾿_(peri22b).mp3
ἐτέλεσσας : "you accomplished, brought to pass", verb 2nd sg aor ind act, from τελέω.

καὶ νῦν ἐν Δαναοῖσι | θεοπροπέων ἀγορεύεις
ὡς δὴ | τοῦδ᾽ ἕνεκά | σφιν ἑκηβόλος ἄλγεα τεύχει

(Hom. Il. 1.109-110)

("And now among the Danaans you claim giving oracles
that for this reason The One Who Strikes From Afar brings woes upon them")

ἀγορεύεις: "you speak in the assembly", verb 2nd sg pres ind act, from ἀγορεύω

θεοπροπέων: "giving oracles", part sg pres act masc nom, part sg pres act masc nom. From θεοπρόπος ("prophecy, oracle, diviner"), composite of θεός ("god, divinity") + πρέπω ("to be clearly visible").

εἰ τό γ᾽ ἄμεινον

(Hom. Il.1.116)

("if that indeed is better")

εἰ: "if"

τό: "that", demonstr. Pronoun., from ὁ, ἡ, τό.

γ᾽ (elided form of γε): "indeed, at any rate", demonstr. Pronoun., from ὁ, ἡ, τό.

ἄμεινον: "better", adj. neut NOM (masc nom: ἀμείνων, gen: -ονος).

ἐπεὶ οὐδὲ ἔοικεν

(Hom. Il.1.119)

("because it doesn't seem right")

ἐπεὶ: "because"

οὐδὲ: "not"

ἔοικεν: "to seem right", perfect, 3rd sg, nu movable, from ἔοικα (perf 1st sg).

: 3rd sg mp impf, from ἀμείβω (note the augment: ε + α = η)

τὸν δ᾽ ἠμείβετ᾽

(Hom. Il.1.121)

("and he replied him")

ἠμείβετ᾽ (elided form of ἠμείβετο): "to respond, reply", 3rd sg imperf. middle, from ἀμείβω. The augment ε + ἀ gives ἠ.
