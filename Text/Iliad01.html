Iliad 1

ἣ μυρί᾽ Ἀχαιοῖς ἄλγε᾽ ἔθηκε,

(Hom. Il. 1.2)

("that countless pains caused to the Acheans")

ἣ: "that, which", relative pronoun, sg fem NOM, from ὅς, ἥ, ὅ.

μυρί᾽ ἄλγε᾽: "countless pains", noun pl neut ACC, elided form of μυρία ἄλγεα.

ἔθηκε: "caused", verb 3rd sg aor ind act, from τίθημι (that can mean: to set, put, place; to put in a certain state, to make, cause).

Ἀχαιοῖς: DAT pl.
