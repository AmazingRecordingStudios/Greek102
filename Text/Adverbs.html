Adverbs

ὅ σφιν ἐὺ φρονέων ἀγορήσατο καὶ μετέειπεν:

(Hom. Il. 1.73)

("Well disposed, he addressed the assembly and told them:")

ἐὺ φρονέων: "well-meaning, well disposed, thinking well". ἐὺ: "well" adv epic indeclform. φρονέων: "thinking, being minded" participle NOM sg, from φρονέω. εὖ is neut. of ἐΰς (adj., "good").

ὅ: NOM sg masc.

σφιν (σφι): "to them, among them", pron. pers DAT pl masc, with nu movable (pl NOM: σφεῖς).

ἀγορήσατο: "he addressed the assembly", verb 3rd sg aor ind mid attic doric ionic aeolic, from verb 3rd sg aor ind mid attic doric ionic aeolic.

μετέειπεν: "speaked among", verb 3rd sg aor ind act epic nu_movable, from μετεῖπον, μετά ("together, among") + εἶπον (aor., "to say, speak").

πόλιν Τροίην εὐτείχεον

(Hom. Il. 1.124)

("The well-walled city of Troy.")

εὐτείχεον: "well-walled, well fortified", adj. ACC sg, from εὐτείχεος, εὐ (adv, "well") + τείχεος ("wall").

εὖ δ᾽ οἴκαδ᾽ ἱκέσθαι

(Hom. Il. 1.19)

("and return safe to your homes.")

ἱκέσθαι: "you come, arrive at, reach", verb aor inf mid.

εὖ: "well", adv.

οἴκαδ᾽ (elided for of οἴκαδε, = οἶκόνδε): "to one's house, home", adv indeclform.

ἐϋκνήμιδες Ἀχαιοί

(Hom. Il. 1.17)

("well-greaved Achaeans")

ἐϋκνήμιδες: "with good greaves", VOC pl masc, from εὐκνήμις (NOM sg), from εὖ ("well") + κνημίς ("greave").

Ἀχαιοί: "Achaeans", VOC pl masc.

ἔνθ᾽ ἄλλοι μὲν πάντες ἐπευφήμησαν Ἀχαιοὶ

(Hom. Il. 1.22)

("Then all the rest of the Achaeans shouted assent")

ἔνθ᾽ (ἔνθα): "thereupon, then, just then" (of time), "there" (of place), adv Demonstr indeclform.

ἐπευφήμησαν "they shouted assent, approved, applauded", verb 3rd pl aor ind act homeric ionic unaugmented, from ἐπευφημέω, ἐπί ("subsequently, after", or simply intensifying the verb) + εὐφημέω ("to cheer, shout in triumph, to applaud"). εὐφημέω in turn is formed with εὐ ("well") + φημί ("to speak").

ὣς ὁ μὲν ἔνθα καθεῦδε

(Hom. Od. 6.1)

("So there he was lying down sleeping.")

ἔνθα: "thereupon, then, just then" (of time), "there" (of place), adv Demonstr indeclform.

καθεῦδε: verb 3rd sg imperf ind act, from καθεύδω.
